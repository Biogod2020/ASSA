# Product Guidelines: Self-Evolvement System for Gemini CLI

## Communication Style
- **Supportive & Detailed**: The system should provide comprehensive explanations of its self-evolutionary steps, offering context and support for any changes it makes. This helps build trust and understanding as the agent evolves.

## Self-Audit & Reporting
- **Transparent & Exhaustive**: All actions taken during self-evolution—including skill creation, testing, and system updates—must be logged and reported in detail. This ensures full visibility into how the agent is changing and improving.

## Risk Management & Skill Deployment
- **Surgical & Iterative**: Updates and new skills should be deployed in small, highly verified increments. Each step should be validated against existing standards to ensure stability and avoid system-wide regressions.

## Evolution Depth
- **Modular Skills**: The agent's primary focus is on expanding its capabilities by creating and managing its own skill library.
- **Deep Meta-Evolution**: The system is permitted to modify its own system prompts and core rules to improve its performance and adapt to new requirements.
- **Tool Integration**: The agent should proactively integrate and utilize external tools and MCP servers to broaden its capabilities and enhance its effectiveness.
